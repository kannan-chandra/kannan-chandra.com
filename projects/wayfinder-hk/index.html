--- layout: project title: Wayfinder HK date: 06-04-2014 ---
 

Wayfinder HK is a mobile app that allows tourists in Hong Kong to quickly find essential public facilities such as medical facilities, police stations, toilets, and consulates. The app was an entry in the 2014 Data.One App Competition.

No Typing

Instantly see the nearest facilites when the app opens. Just swipe.

One Click

Neatly grouped into facility type, with details a single tap away.

You Are Here

Find your way quickly to your destination.

Plan Your Day

Find opening times and plan your day around it.

 
 

Just Cab It

Easily ask for directions or take a cab with the name and address in Chinese.
